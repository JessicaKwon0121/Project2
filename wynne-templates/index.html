What we did

We created an interactive visualization dashboard of key economic indiciators of all US States and counties from Y2015 to Y2019 to better understand the economic valud being contributed to total GDP.

Extract and Transform

Data was extracted from the website of the US Bureau of Economic Activity (BEA) by API request.

Load

We loaded transformed data into MongoDB, and transformed into JSON and then loaded into the database.

Visualizations

We employed JavaScript and associated libraries such as D3.js, Leaflet.js, and Plotly.js. The data for the plots were retrieved from MongoDB via a Flask powered API. This dashboard was created to compare several chart types across different time periods.

Results

The state with the highest GDP is California, and the county with the highest GDP is Los Angeles. From 2015 to 2019 the amount of GDP increased. California, New York, and Texas are leading states.
